## Instructions

1. **Layer**: Add liquids to blender first, then soft ingredients, then frozen items on top.

2. **Blend**: Blend on high until completely smooth, about 1-2 minutes.

3. **Adjust**: Add more liquid if too thick, or ice if too thin.

4. **Serve**: Pour into glasses and enjoy immediately.

## Tips

- Frozen banana makes it creamy without dairy.
- The pineapple sweetness masks the spinach taste.
- Prep smoothie packs in freezer bags for quick mornings.

## Variations

- **Tropical**: Add mango and coconut
- **Berry Green**: Replace pineapple with mixed berries
- **Protein Boost**: Add protein powder or Greek yogurt
