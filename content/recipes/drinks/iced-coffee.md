## Instructions

1. **Brew Coffee**: Brew strong coffee and let it cool completely (or use cold brew).

2. **Sweeten**: Stir in simple syrup or sugar while coffee is still warm if using.

3. **Assemble**: Fill a glass with ice cubes.

4. **Pour**: Add chilled coffee over ice.

5. **Add Milk**: Pour in milk or cream to taste.

6. **Flavor**: Add vanilla extract, caramel, or chocolate syrup if desired. Stir well.

## Tips

- Make coffee ice cubes so your drink doesn't get watered down.
- Cold brew concentrate makes the best iced coffee.
- Batch brew coffee on Sunday for the week ahead.

## Variations

- **Vanilla Sweet Cream**: Mix cream with vanilla and sugar
- **Caramel Macchiato**: Add caramel syrup and vanilla
- **Mocha**: Add chocolate syrup
- **Cinnamon**: Add a dash of cinnamon
