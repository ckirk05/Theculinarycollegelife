## Instructions

1. **Combine Base**: In a jar or container, mix oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, honey, and vanilla.

2. **Refrigerate**: Seal the container and refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours).

3. **Add Toppings**: In the morning, stir and add your favorite toppings like fresh berries, sliced banana, nuts, or a drizzle of nut butter.

4. **Enjoy**: Eat cold or microwave for 30 seconds if you prefer it warm.

## Tips

- Make 3-4 jars at once for meal prep throughout the week.
- Adjust milk amount based on desired thickness.
- Try different flavor combinations like chocolate-banana or apple-cinnamon.

## Variations

- **Berry Blast**: Add mixed berries and a tablespoon of jam
- **Chocolate Peanut Butter**: Add cocoa powder and peanut butter
- **Apple Cinnamon**: Add diced apple and cinnamon
- **Tropical**: Use coconut milk and add mango and pineapple
