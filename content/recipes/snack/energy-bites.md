## Instructions

1. **Mix**: In a bowl, combine all ingredients until well mixed.

2. **Chill**: Refrigerate mixture for 30 minutes to make it easier to handle.

3. **Roll**: Roll mixture into 1-inch balls using your hands.

4. **Store**: Keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 1 week.

## Tips

- Wet your hands slightly to prevent sticking while rolling.
- Customize with dried fruit, coconut, or different nut butters.
- Make a big batch for easy grab-and-go snacks.

## Variations

- **Almond Joy**: Use almond butter, add coconut flakes
- **Cranberry Orange**: Add dried cranberries and orange zest
- **Double Chocolate**: Add cocoa powder for extra chocolate flavor
