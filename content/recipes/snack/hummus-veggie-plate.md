## Instructions

1. **Blend**: In a food processor, combine chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, and salt.

2. **Adjust Texture**: Blend until smooth, adding reserved chickpea liquid (aquafaba) 1 tablespoon at a time until desired consistency.

3. **Finish**: Drizzle in olive oil while processing for extra creaminess.

4. **Serve**: Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and serve with cut vegetables.

## Veggie Suggestions

- Carrots, celery, bell peppers, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, snap peas

## Tips

- Remove chickpea skins for ultra-smooth hummus.
- Store in airtight container in fridge for up to 5 days.
- Top with paprika, pine nuts, or za'atar for variety.
