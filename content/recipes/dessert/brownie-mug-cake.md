## Instructions

1. **Mix Dry**: In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt.

2. **Add Wet**: Add oil, milk, and vanilla. Mix until smooth and no lumps remain.

3. **Add Chips**: Stir in chocolate chips.

4. **Microwave**: Microwave on high for 60-90 seconds. Watch carefully - it will rise up.

5. **Cool**: Let cool for 1 minute before eating (it's hot!).

6. **Serve**: Top with ice cream, whipped cream, or eat straight from the mug.

## Tips

- Don't overmix or overcook for a fudgy texture.
- Cooking time varies by microwave power - start with 60 seconds.
- Perfect for late-night study sessions!

## Variations

- Add a spoonful of peanut butter in the center before microwaving
- Top with caramel sauce
- Mix in mini marshmallows for s'mores flavor
