## Instructions

1. **Cook Quinoa**: Prepare quinoa according to package directions, let cool.

2. **Roast Chickpeas**: Toss chickpeas with olive oil, salt, and roast at 400Â°F for 20 minutes until crispy.

3. **Prep Vegetables**: Chop all vegetables while quinoa and chickpeas cook.

4. **Make Dressing**: Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

5. **Assemble**: Divide quinoa between bowls, top with all ingredients.

6. **Dress and Serve**: Drizzle with dressing and garnish with fresh herbs.

## Tips

- Make components ahead for easy assembly throughout the week.
- Add grilled chicken or salmon for extra protein.
