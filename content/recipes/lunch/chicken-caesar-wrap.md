## Instructions

1. **Prep Ingredients**: Have all ingredients ready and chicken cooked.

2. **Assemble**: Lay tortilla flat, add lettuce down the center, top with chicken.

3. **Add Dressing**: Drizzle Caesar dressing over chicken and lettuce.

4. **Top It Off**: Sprinkle with parmesan and crushed croutons.

5. **Wrap**: Fold in sides, then roll up tightly from bottom.

6. **Slice**: Cut in half diagonally and serve immediately.

## Tips

- Use rotisserie chicken for extra convenience.
- Wrap tightly in parchment paper for easy eating.
- Pack dressing separately if making ahead to prevent sogginess.
